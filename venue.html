Main Venue

T78 Meeting Complex, District 3, Ho Chi Minh City – main hall (600–800 seats), 5 section meeting rooms (45–120 seats), poster and tea-break areas.

Accommodation

On-site rooms: about 250 two-bed rooms at ~USD 40–50 per night. Nearby hotels and guesthouses within walking distance range from USD 17 to 70+ per night.

Travel & Practical Info

  • Major international airport: Tan Son Nhat International Airport (HCMC)
  • Local transport: buses, metro line, taxis, ride-hailing apps (Grab)
  • Currency: VND. Power: 220V AC, 50Hz. Plugs: Type A & F. Emergency: ambulance 115, police 113.
